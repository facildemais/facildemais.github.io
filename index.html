Angry Gran Game


Fullscreen

Experience the excitement of the game that kicked off the legendary Angry Gran series! Step into the shoes of our feisty protagonist, Angry Gran, as she takes to the streets to dispense justice with her trusty weapon. In this action-packed adventure, your mission is to help Angry Gran whack those pesky punk neighbors who are causing trouble in the neighborhood.

With its engaging gameplay and humorous storyline, Angry Gran offers endless fun and challenges. Navigate through various levels, each filled with unique obstacles and adversaries, and unleash Gran's fury on the unsuspecting punks. Collect coins and power-ups along the way to boost your abilities and achieve high scores.

Whether you're a longtime fan of the Angry Gran series or a newcomer, this game promises to deliver hours of entertainment and laughter. Share your high scores with friends and family, and see who can help Gran achieve the ultimate victory. Don't miss out on the game that started it all—play Angry Gran today and join the adventure!
